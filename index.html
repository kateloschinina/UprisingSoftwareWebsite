About

I've known Ruby On Rails since 2006, and professionally started using it in 2008. Since then variety of different size companies have hired me, from small crafty startup to bigger corporate companies where i've learnt to follow a diverse range of processes.

The ruby ecosystem is well known to me, and i had a few chance contribute to a few libraries and started my own. Contribution to open source software is something i would like to see companies to encourage more within their teams.

On boarding new team members is something i've always enjoyed doing and is also a great way to get to know your new colleagues and make them feel welcome within the team.
